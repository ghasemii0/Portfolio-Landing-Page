About Me:

I am from Iran and I am here since 2010. I left Iran 2008 and lived in Turkey for two years. After I came to the United States I attended PCC and took some courses there . My favorite course at PCC was C++ .I got my Associate degree and left school. My job experiences in order are:

  1. Seller at the clothes shop
  2. Working at PCC coffeteria
  3. Fixing iPhones
  4. Running Ebay store
  5. Real estate agent
  6. Driving for ridesharing companies

After working on those jobs for years it was time for change. I didn't know what carrier to choose after, but I knew if I want to have a better job I need to educate myself, but I didn't want to go back to school for another two years and spend anothe $30,000. One day when I was driving for uber a rider told me about coding bootcamps and after I did my research I decided to join Epicodus.

When I was in Turkey I lived in a town called Cappadocia. Every morning there was more than hundreds of hot air bollons in the sky.
